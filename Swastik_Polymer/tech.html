Research & Development

Our R&D team at Swastik Polymers is composed of experienced engineers, chemical analysts, and designers who bring a wealth of scientific and practical knowledge in footwear technology. With their expertise, every project is executed seamlessly—from initial sketches to the creation of production-ready sole samples—in minimal time. At Swastik Polymers, we are dedicated to setting new benchmarks in sole manufacturing with innovation, precision, and excellence at the forefront.

Production

At Swastik Polymers, we operate advanced production lines dedicated to ARI mix and TPR soles, supported by state-of-the-art Italian machinery, including rotary and banana moulding machines, washing machines, spray machines, and a lean assembly line. Our in-house mixing plant ensures superior quality materials for sole production, while an extensive collection of moulds enables fast and efficient sample development. Our skilled team of engineers, supervisors, and laborers excels in executing orders on time, maintaining strict quality standards at every stage of production. This commitment to excellence ensures our soles consistently stand out in the industry.

Quality Control

At Swastik Polymers, quality control is at the core of our operations. Every stage of production, from material preparation to the final product, undergoes meticulous inspection to ensure it meets the highest industry standards. Our dedicated quality assurance team utilizes advanced testing techniques and tools to monitor durability, consistency, and finish. By maintaining strict control measures, we ensure that each sole not only meets but exceeds customer expectations, reinforcing our reputation for reliability and excellence in the industry.
